ABOUT IEEE

IEEE is the biggest organization for professional collaborations in the world. It is dedicated towards advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members work in synchronisation to inspire a global community through IEEE’s highly cited and acclaimed publications, conferences, technology standards as well as through its professional and educational activities.





ABOUT IEEE DELHI SECTION

IEEE Delhi Section is an intricately woven technical community that falls under Asia-Pacific Region, the Region 10 of IEEE. It is a bumbling count of precisely 88 Student branches, 18 student chapters and 4781 active Members (including 21 Society Affiliates). The section is also graced by 7 Life Fellow members, 7 Fellow members, 33 Life Senior members, 357 Senior members, 7 Life members, 48 Associate Member, 793 Graduate Student Member and one SA 2018 Membership.

IEEE Delhi Section spans over major areas of northern India viz. National Capital Territory of Delhi, Union Territory of Chandigarh and the five states of Rajasthan, Haryana, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh and Jammu & Kashmir. Foundation of IEEE Delhi Section was laid on May 13, 1976. Since then it has always strung along with the regulations, standards and by-laws set by IEEE and has worked in sync with IEEE’s international community.

Eighteen society/council chapters along with the affinity groups i.e. IEEE Young Professionals (YP) and Women in Engineering (WIE) together envision the same objective - “Advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity”. To transform this vision into practical reality the Delhi Section consistently works towards bringing together events such as workshops, seminars, symposiums, tech talks, tech fests etc. The responsibilities of the section don’t just end with bringing together most sought after professionals, techies, scientists, researchers and freelancers together on a stage. Rather it extends to understanding the technical inclination of its members and embracing the individual growth and development of each of its components.

Continuously transitioning boundaries are the new normal. IEEE Delhi has always believed in erasing the benchmark it sets each year and working tirelessly towards setting a new higher one. With the professional affiliation and association of all its member branches, the section’s efforts will be met with equivalent zeal and ardour.

Events

Past Events

IEEE DSC 2019

12-13 October, 2019  IIIT Delhi

IEEE Delhi Section Student Congress 2019 is IEEE Delhi section’s biggest assembly of engineers. Attracting hundreds of delegates from more than 50 Student Branches, DSC is an annual event that provides a great opportunity to reach out and engage students from every institution in the field of Engineering. Delhi Section Student Congress is a magnificent opportunity, an edifying experience and an invaluable platform for delegates to collaborate and ideate. DSC 2019 was held at IIIT Delhi on 12th and 13th october, 2019. This year DSC’s theme was “INSPIRING INCLUSIVE INNOVATION”

IEEE RSC 2019

12 January, 2020  Manipal University, Jaipur

Equal inclusion and participation of all subsections is necessary to create an opportunity for students to collaborate with and learn from various industry partners, sponsors and other student branches. Hence on 12th January, 2020, the first ever Rajasthan Sub section congress was held in Manipal University, Jaipur.

IEEE Delhi Section AGM 2020

22 Feb, 2020  NSUT, Delhi

Every organization works with respect to a framework. And the framework requires communication. IEEE Delhi Section Annual General Meeting gathers all the decision makers and torchbearers of the section for a review of the annual progress and for throwing light on future plans. This year’s AGM was conducted on 22nd Feb, 2020 in NSUT, Delhi.
